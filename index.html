    FAQ

    What types of clothing can I rent?

    We offer a wide range of clothing items, including dresses, tops, skirts, pants, jackets, and accessories such as jewelry and handbags.

    How long can I rent an item for?

    Rental periods can vary, but they typically range from a few days to a few weeks. Some rental fashion businesses also offer longer rental periods for special occasions.

    Will it be possible to trial Rent-A-Fashion for one month?

    Yes, you can easily pause or cancel your subscription at any time, we’re 100% flexible.

    What happens if I damage or lose an item?

    We require customers to pay a fee if an item is lost or damaged beyond normal wear and tear. The fee can vary depending on the item and the extent of the damage.

    What happens if I need to return an item late?

    We charge a late fee for items that are returned after the agreed-upon rental period. The late fee can vary depending on the length of the delay.
